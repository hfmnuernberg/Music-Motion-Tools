Music Motion Tools Demo

⚠️ Music Motion Tools uses the Camera. There is no recording!

🏠 Hand Tracking is computed localy on this Machine.

Instructions:

🎥 To start click the Camera Button in the top left corner.

👋 Show your hands to the camera. The index finger tips are marked red.

🔴 You can play by placing the red dots into the blue bars by moving your hands

🔊 Choose your instrument by clicking Metallophone, Xylophone or Hand Pan

🎼 Click one of the exercise buttons and try to hit the bars in time with the moving blocks

😊 Enjoy playing!
